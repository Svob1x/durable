About us

Welcome to TJ Těšnovice, a Football Club located in Těšnovice, Zlínský kraj. We are dedicated to promoting the love for football and fostering a strong sense of community among our members.

With a passion for the sport and a commitment to excellence, we strive to provide a positive and inclusive environment for players of all levels. Join us in pursuing our shared goal of enjoying the beautiful game and growing together as a team.
